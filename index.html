Mythotech

A student-led nonprofit organization aiming to bring innovators and creative individuals in the field of technology together.

Join Today

Why are we unqiue?

Mythotech connects creatives all over, regardless of race, religion, gender, socioeconomic status, or background.

What do we do?

We develop and support efforts to expose cutting-edge technology to high school students. Technology includes ChatGPT, GPT-4, Stable Diffusion, and more.

What do we provide?

We provide interactive and immersive learning workshops for members and also work and connect with industry professionals to provide the best possible workshops and resources.

Empowering minds, igniting change, and forging futures - this is what Mythotech is about.

Advita Bathole, President and Co-Founder of Mythotech
